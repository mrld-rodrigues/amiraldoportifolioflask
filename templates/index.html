IEFP- FINAL COURSE PROJECT

It is an app developed with Python, MariaDB, and Kivy that helps entomological technicians handle, store, and generate data for analysis related to entomological research. It also serves as an indicator for actions against vector reproduction and disease transmission, generating key indices to measure egg positivity and density, as well as adult females.
